c. Information from Third Parties

  • SurePass API: Aadhaar verification results (e.g., OTP status).
  • PhonePe: Payment confirmations and transaction IDs.
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide services like authentication, Aadhaar verification, property management, UPI payments, notifications, and analytics.
  • Enhance security with masked Aadhaar and Firebase rules.
  • Improve the App via usage analytics.
  • Send in-app notifications for payments and bookings.
  • Comply with UIDAI, IT Act, 2000, and other laws.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share it:

  • With service providers (SurePass, PhonePe, Firebase) under confidentiality agreements.
  • For legal obligations (e.g., court orders, UIDAI audits).
  • With your consent (e.g., sharing property details with renters).

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Data is stored in Firebase Firestore with encryption.
  • Security: Firebase Authentication, Firestore rules, and Cloud Functions protect data. Regular audits ensure integrity.
  • Retention: Data is kept only as needed (e.g., payment records for 5 years). Request deletion via contact (Section 7).

5. Third-Party Services

SurePass (Aadhaar), PhonePe (payments), and Firebase (authentication, analytics) process data under their privacy policies. We do not store full Aadhaar numbers or sensitive payment details.

  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics in settings (when available).
